Welcome to my home bridal wear service.

If you are struggling to book a fitting; working at the weekends or have family that are unable to join you then trying your wedding dress in the comfort of your own home may be right for you.

Home Bridle Wear


I offer a personalised service that allows you to try a variety of wedding dresses in the comfort of your own home. I work in conjunction with a number of local bridal wear shops in the central belt. Please use the links below to browse the selection of dresses held by each of the companies.

My service is simple, flexible and can allow you to enjoy this special occasion in the comfort of your own home with friends and family.

Your Dress

You may have some idea of the type of dress you would like or you may have no idea. If you do not have a specific style in mind I can select a number of dresses, which cover a range of bases.

All you have to is let me know the types of designers or style of dress and we can work together to find the perfect dress for you.

Your Date

Once you have an idea of the types of dress you would like you can book an appointment by following the link below. All I need is a few personal details and a date that is suitable for you.

You might work at the weekends and feel that a Thursday evening would more convenient for you. We can have a chat about you interest and what would work best for you.

Your Personalised Fitting

I shall bring a full length mirror, sample dresses and some bubbles for your friends and family to enjoy. I charge a small service charge of £50 (which covers transport costs, sourcing the dresses and bubbles).

The good news is that the service charge will be deducted from the cost of the dress if you find the right one. Please use the link below to book an appointment. I look forward to hearing from you.
